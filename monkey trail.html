ANIMALS MAP PLACES

MONKEY TRAIL

Follow a lively and playful trail filled with monkey species from across the globe. See them swing, jump, and play through ropes and branches.

  • Howler monkeys, capuchins, and spider monkeys
  • Feeding times at 10h00am and 15h00pm
  • Photo zone and viewing decks available

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Monkey Trail starts south of the Lost Forest and loops around the central lake.
